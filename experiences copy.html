My Experiences

Photography

Ever since I got my first camera for Christmas in 2018, I have been in love with photography. Over the years I’ve improved my skills, to the point where I am satisfied with the quality of the pictures I am taking. Because of my passion for photography, I have developed an eye for unique and creative compositions, even when I am not actively taking pictures. I can now better capture the blissful beauty of life and nature, without putting much thought into it. My favorite categories of photography are animal, architectural, portrait, and nature.
