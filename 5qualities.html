The Five Qualities

Collaboration

Collaboration is when people work together towards a common goal, combining their efforts, skills, and ideas. It involves cooperating, sharing responsibilities, and communicating effectively to achieve a shared outcome or solve a problem collectively.

Communication

Communication is the process of sharing information or ideas between people in a way that they can understand each other. It involves using words, gestures, or other means to express thoughts, feelings, or instructions, enabling effective interaction and mutual understanding.

Creativity

Creativity is the skill to come up with new and valuable ideas or ways of doing things that go beyond what's usual. It means thinking in different and unique ways, putting together different things, and imagining new possibilities, which can lead to fresh and important results.

Persistence

Persistence is the quality of not giving up easily and continuing to work towards a goal, even when faced with challenges, setbacks, or obstacles. It involves staying determined, showing perseverance, and maintaining a strong effort over time, ultimately increasing the likelihood of achieving desired results.

Inquiry

Inquiry is the process of asking questions, seeking information, and exploring a subject in a curious and systematic manner. It involves being inquisitive, open-minded, and actively engaging with a topic to gain knowledge, deepen understanding, or discover new insights.
